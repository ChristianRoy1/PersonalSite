Project Background

I've always had an interest in the technology surrounding websites, mobile apps, and the design philosophy needed to create a great user experience. Over the past years it's clear that the line between a typical mobile app and a well designed website has blurred significantly. Along with this came an increased ease of accesbility for any individual to create an app-like experience that can work across various kinds of devices. With my interest peaking, I decided to try and self-teach myself a bit about the world of web development and try my hand at creating a few app 'lite' projects.

An obvious first step included creating a pilot project to kickstart the learning process and what better project than creating a profile webpage with a tag-along page to highlight my subsequent projects.
